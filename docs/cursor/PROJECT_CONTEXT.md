Sonia's journey began with personal healing — discovering that vibrational sound could regulate the nervous system when talk and movement alone could not. What started as intimate gong immersions and sound baths evolved into global retreat experiences, corporate wellness programs, and private sessions for leaders and seekers alike.

### Key Milestones

- Deep training in sound therapy and gong mastery

- Corporate wellness partnerships with executive teams

## Design Philosophy

Create feelings of: **calm · spaciousness · trust · luxury · stillness · transformation**

Never cluttered. Use large typography, generous whitespace, slow animations, cinematic imagery, elegant transitions.

Avoid: loud gradients, neon colors, aggressive animations, generic yoga illustrations, stock wellness clichés.
