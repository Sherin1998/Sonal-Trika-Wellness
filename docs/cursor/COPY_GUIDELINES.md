# COPY GUIDELINES — Trika Yoga & Wellness

## Content Structure (Mandatory)

**Never describe the service first.** Every page, section, and landing page must follow this sequence:

| Step | What to Write | Example |
|------|---------------|---------|
| 1. Problem | The underlying condition | Nervous system trapped in chronic stress |
| 2. Symptom | What the reader experiences | Sleep suffers, clarity fades, body struggles to recover |
| 3. Desired transformation | The state they move toward | Deep restoration, physiological calm |
| 4. Methodology | How Sonia guides them | Immersive sound healing with planetary gongs and vibrational frequencies |
| 5. CTA | Invitational next step | Begin Your Healing Journey |

### Bad vs Good

**Bad:**
> "We offer sound healing sessions."

**Good:**
> "When the nervous system remains trapped in chronic stress, sleep suffers, clarity fades, and the body struggles to recover. Through immersive sound healing experiences using planetary gongs and vibrational frequencies, clients are guided into deep states of restoration and physiological calm."

This structure applies to: homepage sections, service pages, landing pages, corporate copy, and CTAs. See `LANDING_PAGE_STRATEGY.md` for page-specific angles.

## Voice & Tone

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Warm | Approachable, never cold or clinical |
| Compassionate | Acknowledge pain without pity |
| Grounded | Rooted in science and experience, not mysticism |
| Wise | Confident guide, not guru |
| Premium | Elevated language without pretension |
| Human | Real, relatable, never performative |

**Write as:** A modern healer and trusted guide — not a salesperson or spiritual influencer.

## Words & Phrases to Avoid

### Spiritual Clichés

- "Ancient secrets"
- "Journey to enlightenment"
- "Manifest abundance"
- "Raise your vibration"
- "Unlock your potential"
- "Transform your life"
- "Awaken your inner goddess/warrior"

### Corporate Jargon

- "Synergy"
- "Leverage"
- "Best-in-class"
- "Holistic solutions" (overused)
- "Disrupt"
- "ROI-driven wellness"

### Generic Wellness

- "Find your zen"
- "Namaste vibes"
- "Good vibes only"
- "Self-care Sunday"
- "You deserve this" (overused)

## Headline Patterns

### Hero Headlines

Evocative, spacious, emotionally resonant. One powerful idea per headline.

**Good examples:**

- "When the body feels safe, healing begins."
- "Sound that restores what stress has taken."
- "Stillness is not the absence of noise. It is the return to yourself."

**Avoid:**

- "Welcome to Trika Yoga & Wellness"
- "Your Journey Starts Here"
- "Transform Your Life Today"

### Section Headlines

Clear, benefit-oriented, grounded.

**Good:**

- "Programs for Leaders Under Pressure"
- "What a Gong Immersion Feels Like"
- "Retreats Designed for Deep Restoration"

---

## Body Copy Rules

1. **Short paragraphs** — 2–4 sentences max
2. **Lead with empathy** — acknowledge the reader's state before offering solution
3. **Be specific** — "90-minute gong immersion" not "healing session"
4. **Science + spirit balance** — mention nervous system alongside vibrational healing
5. **No exclamation marks** — calm confidence, not hype
6. **Second person sparingly** — "you" is fine; avoid overuse

## Audience-Specific Tone

### Corporate Leaders

Professional warmth. Acknowledge pressure without dramatizing. Focus on outcomes: focus, resilience, team cohesion, sustainable performance.

**Example:** "Leadership demands constant output. These programs create space for nervous system recovery — so clarity and presence return naturally."

### Wellness Seekers

Gentle, invitational. Validate their experience. Emphasize safety and gradual transformation.

**Example:** "If exhaustion has become your baseline, you are not alone. Sound healing offers a path back to inner safety — without forcing change."

### Retreat Participants

Evocative, immersive. Paint the experience. Nature, stillness, depth.

**Example:** "Three days where the only agenda is restoration. Mountains, silence, and the resonance of the gong — held in a container designed for deep letting go."
